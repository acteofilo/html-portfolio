My Web Development Career Journey

I've alway found technology intriguing, and I get it. It's exciting.
I just didn't think I could make it in this field. It felt like it
was beyond my reach.
I grew up in Canada with immigrant parents that were not able to
provide support or encouragement and so I always thought that
it just wasn't for me, although I was always reading articles about
the what how and why of anything technological, go figure!
I finally figured out that it is for me and I'm actually good at it!

This is My Story

There is no stopping me. My determination to succeed is imesurable.
As I grow older different versions of myself emerge.
And with every version, I want something more, something different.
I could live a comfortable life with what I have accomplished
and just continue doing the same job for the rest of my life,
but the passion was gone.
I became disatisfied, uncontent, unhappy.
I find joy and excitement in the unconfortable and so...
Now that I'm older, I decided to start over..go back to University...well not
back to - but for the first time actually, with students twice my age.
It was an amazing experience!
This is my journey... with no appologies.

My Academic Journey

I graduated with a degree in Commerce with a major in
Business Technology Management from Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU)
During my time there, I was also working at finishing up
with my previous career while also working for the Government of Ontario,
as an Executive IT Officer Intern for 8 months, where I gained extensive
knowledge in applications administration and troubleshooting
amongst other things. It was a great learning experience, but the passion was missing,
and so my journey continueed for the search of something I could be passionate about.
I'm not of the type to do something just to do it, I need to love what I do.

My Career Journey

Before attending TMU, I had exceptional success in contributing to the
substantial growth of a company in the beauty industry for 16 years.
I provided services to clients that exceeded expectation.
With a 500-client base, I created a very successful career. In addition,
I also managed and contributed to all aspects of the business, such as
training, education and contributing to the creation of promotional
advertising within the company, including managing social media accounts.

Then came that point in your life where you want something really
different...
another version of yourself emerges..
I decided to leave my old life and persue my fascination with technology.
I wasn't sure which route was best for me but I always dabbled in coding,
but always got a negative connotation whenever I looked at people who code
( I was obvioulsy looking in the wrong place!)
- and so developed a Web Developer dream ;).

Of course, I couldn't have done it all without the assistance of my dog Lucy!
